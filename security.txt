# Security Policy for Credli.ai

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

1. Email us at security@credli.ai with details
2. Allow reasonable time for us to respond (typically 48-72 hours)
3. Do not publicly disclose until we've had time to address the issue

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will acknowledge security researchers who help improve our platform's security.

# Scope
This security policy applies to:
- https://credli.ai (main site)
- All subdomains of credli.ai
- Associated web applications and services

# Out of Scope
- Social engineering attacks
- Denial of service attacks
- Physical security issues

Thank you for helping keep Credli.ai secure.
